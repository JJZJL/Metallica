Upstanders and Bystanders

Bullying can occur between one person or another, or a group of people. 'Bystanders' play

an important role in either preventing bullying or allowing bullying to continue

What is a Bystander?

A bystander is someone whe witnesses bullying, either in person or online

A bystander is someone can be:

  • Friends
  • Students
  • Teachers
  • School staff
  • Parents
  • Coaches
  • Other adults

    • What is an Upstander?

    An Upstander is someone who witnesses bulying and intervenes or speaks up to stop the bullying
