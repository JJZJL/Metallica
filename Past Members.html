Metallicas Past Guitarist

1-Dave Mustaine

Dave Mustaine is the only past metallica guitarist. These days Dave tours with his band MEGADETH."they could have ended up making twinky bars for all I cared but I still would have wished I was with them, there was this feel of brotherhood when I was with the band" said Dave Mustaine.

Dave Started the band MEGADETH, which became one of the most popular Thrash Metal Bands, and joined Metallica in the the position of The Big 4 which is made up of Metallica, Megadeth, Anthrax and Slayer

2-Cliff Burton

on the 27th of September 1986 Cliff Lee Burton died in a tradgic bus crash. His last known words were "Maybe I Will"

The bus driver, claimed to have slipped on black ice, however James Found the drivers claim, total BS. James Recalled the night; "I Walked for miles looking for help and I never saw any sighn of black ice, that bus driver was drunk" While all of the crew and the rest of the band members were relatively unharmed, most sustaining cuts and bruises with a few broken bones, Cliff Died, The window he was sleeping next to was weakened from the cold and when the bus toppled on its side, cliff fell out of the vehicle and was crushed by the bus

Cliff was replaced rather quickly by Jason Newstead."We had a funeral for Cliff then we got a new bassist and kept on going, I suppose that all we knew how to do" Said Lars in an interview with Brian Johnson (Lead Vocal of the hard rock band AC/DC)

3-Jason Newstead

Jason Newstead was with the band for 15 years before leaving, "I would wake up and find shaving cream in my bed" recalls Jason in the Documentry Hired Gun. "Even though they were pranking me, I knew that they were really just making sure I could get used to these kind of things, cause you know life on the road is tough"

Jason's bass lines on his first metallica album were acually removed in studio as the rest of the band didn't really trust Jasons bass skills. When Jason heard this he was enranged but he still stayed with the band.
