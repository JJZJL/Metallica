Metallica a Brief History

Metallica is a Thrash Metal band formed in 1981 by Lars Ulrich and James Hetfield

The Original band was made up of James Hetfield, Lars Ulrich, Dave Mustaine

(who later Formed the Band MEGADETH) and Ron Ron Mcgovney

  • James Hetfield: Rhythm Guitar
  • Lars Ulrich: drums
  • Dave Mustaine: Lead Guitar
  • Ron Ron Mcgovney: Bass

    • Ron Mcgovney was Fired after James and Lars found the bassist:Cliff Burton

    Cliff tragically died in a tour bus accident during thier Master of Puppets Tour and was replaced quickly by Jason Newstead

    Newstead went on to serve the Band for another 15 year before ultimatley quitting due to creative differences

    When Newstead Left He was replaced by Rober Tujilo who is the current bassist
